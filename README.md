# About Me

👋 Hi, I’m [@thomaswitt](https://github.com/thomaswitt), a tech entrepreneur,
angel investor, and co-founder of
[Expedite Ventures](https://www.expedite.ventures/). With 25+ years in tech, my
passion is building, scaling, and advising software companies.

My journey began with computers at age 8. I discovered the internet in 1990 and
co-founded my first software business in 1997, right after high school, where I
served as CTO, planning and developing complex B2B SaaS products. Since then,
I've remained deeply involved in software engineering and tech strategy. I've
been an enthusiastic Ruby on Rails developer since 2009.

[Expedite Ventures](https://www.expedite.ventures/) is a business angel group of
CTOs and CPOs. We invest in exceptional deep tech founders and help them to
create sustainable businesses through both (pre-)seed capital and in-depth,
hands-on mentoring, leveraging our decades of collective expertise. If you're a
tech founder interested in pitching us, please check out our
[Playbook](https://www.expedite.ventures/playbook) first.

I also offer specialized tech due diligence and advisory services to private
equity and VC firms.

Outside work, I enjoy making music, flying, sailing, photography, and cooking.
